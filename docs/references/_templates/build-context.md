## Showability Gate

Before building, answer:

- Would Jorge be comfortable showing the first viewport to the business owner?
- Does this feel specific to the client, not just the category?
- Does the design serve trust rather than visual ego?
- Is the primary conversion action obvious?
- Are there any fake claims, fake proof, or unsupported medical outcomes?

If any answer fails, do not build yet.
